Patient Reactivation

Patient hasn't booked in 60–90 days? An automated, personal-feeling message goes out with a relevant reason to come back — seasonal health hook, treatment follow-up, check-in. Lapsed patients come back without a single manual outreach.

Appointment Reminders

Patient booked → reminder at 24h and 2h before. Practitioner name, clinic address, what to bring. No-show rates drop. Front desk spends less time on confirmation calls.

Review Request

Appointment complete → automated message with a direct Google review link. Sent at exactly the right moment, when the visit is fresh. Most clinics have hundreds of happy patients who've never been asked.
