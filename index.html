How It Works

We transform your webinar into a powerful lead magnet in three seamless steps. First, we collect your webinar recording, slides, brand guidelines, target audience details, and desired CTAs to align with your goals. Next, our team transcribes the content, creates a structured outline, designs branded visuals, and crafts a 16–24-page enhanced ebook optimized for lead generation with SEO keywords and external links. Finally, we deliver the ebook as a PDF with source files, and for Launch and Scale clients, we provide a mobile-optimized landing page and pre-written X/LinkedIn post templates to capture names, emails, and company details, seamlessly syncing with your CRM.

Quality and Accuracy are paramount, our process includes quality checkpoints to ensure first pass success, however we recognize the need for changes and revisions and will stand behind our work.

  • Content Quality Scorecard & Traceability Map (timestamps ↔ slide IDs).
  • Red-Pen review and “Before → After” ensuring no conflicts of messaging, eliminates mis-spoken details, errors, omissions from the webinar or slides
  • Guarantees: On-Brand voice and messaging. True alignment with original content
